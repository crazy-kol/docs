# Get Started with Syntax Studios

Ready to bring your vision to life? Follow our simple three-step process to get your project started with our expert development team.

## Step 1: Contact Us

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="envelope" title="Reach out to our team">
    Get in touch with us through our preferred contact methods:
    
    - **Email**: [contact@syntaxstudios.com](mailto:contact@syntaxstudios.com)
    - **Discord**: Join our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/syntaxstudios)
    - **GitHub**: Check out our [GitHub profile](https://github.com/syntaxstudios)
    
    <Tip>We typically respond within 24 hours!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="clipboard-list" title="Tell us about your project">
    When contacting us, please include:
    
    - Project type (FiveM, Discord, Web Development, or combination)
    - Timeline and budget expectations
    - Specific features or requirements
    - Any existing code or assets you have
    
    <Tip>The more details you provide, the better we can help you!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step 2: Project Planning

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="users" title="Consultation call">
    We'll schedule a consultation call with our founders to discuss your project in detail:
    
    - **Matty** - UX/UI Design and user experience
    - **kdawg** - Technical architecture and development
    - **Peter** - Project strategy and business requirements
    
    This ensures we understand your vision completely before we start building.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="file-contract" title="Project proposal">
    After our consultation, we'll provide you with:
    
    - Detailed project scope and timeline
    - Transparent pricing breakdown
    - Development milestones and deliverables
    - Support and maintenance options
    
    <Tip>No surprises - everything is clearly outlined upfront!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step 3: Development & Launch

<Accordion icon="rocket" title="Build and deploy">
  Once you approve the proposal:
  
  1. **Development begins** - Our team starts building your solution
  2. **Regular updates** - We keep you informed with progress reports
  3. **Testing & refinement** - Thorough testing ensures quality
  4. **Launch & support** - We deploy your project and provide ongoing support
  
  <Tip>We maintain open communication throughout the entire process!</Tip>
</Accordion>
